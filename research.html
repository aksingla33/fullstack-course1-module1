Guided the following for Ph.D.:

  • Krishna H S (2016): High-tech Startup Lifecycle in India: An Exploratory Study of the Determinants of Emergence, Survival and Growth. The thesis has been submitted for the Ph.D. degree of Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore in July 2016. The thesis is currently under evaluation.

The thesis was awarded Ph.D. degree by Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore in March 2006. Awarded the Indian Economic Association Gold Medal in 2007. (Joint Guide: Dr P Balachandra).
  • N V Raghavendra (2003): Acquisition of Technological Capability and Economic Performance in Clusters: A Study of Belgaum and Coimbatore Small Scale Foundries. The thesis was awarded Ph.D. by Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore in July 2003.
